About BhargavaNiveda Consulting

BhargavaNiveda Consulting is a distinguished multinational professional services firm headquartered in Bangalore, India. Established in 2024 by renowned accountant Nindra Rathna Manjusha, the firm has quickly emerged as a trusted name in the global professional services landscape.

With a comprehensive portfolio that includes audit, consulting, financial advisory, risk advisory, tax, and legal services, BhargavaNiveda is committed to delivering high-impact solutions that drive sustainable growth and operational excellence for clients worldwide.

Supported by a team of approximately 50 dedicated professionals across the globe, BhargavaNiveda Consulting combines deep industry expertise with a forward-thinking approach to help organizations navigate complexity and seize opportunities in an ever-evolving business environment.
